Kodak Case Study

From Dominance to Decline • ISPS Course Presentation

Overview

This case study analyzes Kodak’s downfall despite being an early innovator in digital imaging. It explores internal rigidity, missed opportunities, and external pressures from digital competitors. The study highlights lessons for modern companies navigating technological disruption.

  • Course: ISPS (Information Systems Policy & Strategy)
  • Focus: Strategic missteps, digital disruption, organizational culture
  • Format: Group presentation with analysis slides
