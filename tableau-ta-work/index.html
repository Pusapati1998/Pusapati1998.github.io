Santa Clara University · Business Intelligence & Data Warehousing (Teaching Assistant)

Overview

As part of my role as a Teaching Assistant for the Business Intelligence & Data Warehousing course, I taught Tableau to graduate and undergraduate students. This included lectures, demos, and hands-on projects, guiding students in building dashboards to highlight anomalies, patterns, and KPIs for informed decision-making.

  • Topics: Tableau basics, dashboards, BI cycle, data fields, chart types, KPI reporting.
  • Tech: Tableau, SQL, BI concepts, data visualization best practices.
