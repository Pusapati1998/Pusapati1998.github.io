Candy Data Tableau Project

Visual Analytics • Exploratory Analysis • Sales Insights

Overview

This Tableau project analyzed U.S. candy distributor data (2021–2024) to identify sales drivers, seasonal patterns, and regional performance. Dashboards combine KPIs, maps, Pareto analysis, and scatter plots to support strategic recommendations for logistics, pricing, and promotions.

  • Dataset: Candy distribution dataset from Maven Analytics
  • Team: Padma Rishitha Pusapati and group members
  • Goal: Improve sales performance, shipping efficiency, and product profitability

Key Dashboards & Insights

  • KPI Summary: Total Sales, Avg Order Value, Customer Count, Production Volume.
  • Regional Map: Sales by state and region, highlighting California and New York as top markets.
  • Pareto Analysis: A small set of top products (mainly chocolates) drive most revenue (80/20 rule).
  • Stability Scatter: Differentiates reliable products from volatile ones.
  • Sales Trends: Strong seasonal spikes in Q4 (holiday demand).

Strategic Recommendations

  • Focus promotions in Q4 to capitalize on holiday spikes.
  • Adjust pricing for high-sales but low-margin products.
  • Expand high-performing chocolates into untapped regions.
  • Improve logistics in regions with shipping inefficiencies.

Business Impact

These insights help the distributor optimize pricing, regional campaigns, and inventory strategy. By aligning operations with customer demand patterns, the business can maximize profitability and customer satisfaction.
