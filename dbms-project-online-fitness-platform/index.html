Online Fitness Platform

Database Design · EER Model · Memberships · Sessions · Payments

Overview

A database-backed platform for fitness services supporting personalized workout plans, nutrition guidance, trainer interactions, payments, BMI tracking, referrals, and feedback. Core entities include Login, Customer, Trainer, Membership, WorkoutPlan, ExercisePlan, Exercises, Session, NutritionPlan, Payment, BMI, and Feedback.

Key Features

  • Personalized workout & nutrition plans
  • Membership tiers (online to premium with offline training)
  • Trainer sessions, feedback & ratings
  • Secure payments with referral discounts
  • BMI calculation & progress tracking
